About Arun,

Consistently passionate about mathematics and aptitude, Arun holds double masters in mathematics and has 11 years of continued experience at Ralli Government School in Mansa District of Punjab and has been working with the State Board (PSEB) as a Subject Expert for 2 years now.
Innovation in simplifying ideas and methods to solve mathematical puzzles has been his forte which has always stood wonderfully with his students who found themselves struggling with it. Being one of the trickiest subject, mathematics has always been a nightmare for many to deal with. But with his ways of teaching it has always been quite playful and fun for students to learn it.

Objective of Application

This app has been developed particularly for students to meet their requirements in a simplified manner, and also for the teachers to relate to the new methods and strategies incorporated in the app helping them to understand student’s problem in a more realistic manner. It will help students during the course of time in complexities they face while solving the problems.

For Teachers

The simple layout will help teachers to teach students in a systematic manner. Day-to-day designed schedules (including topics) will help in more efficient teaching and obtaining best results with less labor.

For Students

A 24X7 helping hand for the students bringing in more confidence. Students won’t need to go long distances and pay heavy amounts for tuition fees anywhere now. This is the substitute of your tuitions in your hands.

This app introduces an all new Expert Section which brings mathematics experts from different Districts of Punjab within the reach of students to sort out their problems and queries in matter of few minutes now.
